# Commonly and frequently aksed questions and comments for Vein, the game.

***

## Q: How can I access the admin menu?

## A: Pressing `\` will bring up the admin menu.  If you are on a QWERTY keyboard with a different layout, you may be required to use `Insert` on your keyboard.  If you do not see this or cannot map your binding to `Insert`, you will need to manually map it in your GameUserSettings.ini file located in the same path as your `Engine.ini` and `Game.ini` files

***
## Q: How can I toggle a photo mode?  Can I remove the item names or remove my HUD entirely?

## A: Pressing `~` on your keyboard to bring up `developer mode`, you then then use the following command to remove all items on your HUD: `ShowHUD` which is a toggle for on/off

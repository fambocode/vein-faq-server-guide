# Commonly and frequently aksed questions and comments for Vein, the game.

***

## Q: How can I access the admin menu?

## A: Pressing `\` will bring up the admin menu.  

If you are on a QWERTY keyboard with a different layout, you may be required to use `Insert` on your keyboard.  

If you do not see this or cannot map your binding to `Insert`, you will need to manually map it in your `GameUserSettings.ini` file located in the same path as your other configuration files;
- `Engine.ini`
- `Game.ini`

***
## Q: How can I toggle a photo mode?  Can I remove the item names or remove my HUD entirely?

## A: Pressing `~` on your keyboard and using the `ShowHUD` command.

Once you see the console, typing `ShowHUD` will toggle the feature on or off.
